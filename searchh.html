Google The most widely used search engine with extensive web index and integrated services. Bing Known for visual and image search, integrated with Microsoft products. Yahoo A blend of search engine and news portal with email services. DuckDuckGo Focuses on privacy with no tracking and a clean interface. Ecosia Uses search revenue to plant trees and protect the environment. Naver Popular in South Korea, offering blogs, news, and cafes. Daum Integrated with Kakao services, popular in South Korea. Baidu China's largest search engine with local market dominance. Yandex Russia's Google equivalent with maps and email services. Seznam Czech Republic's favorite search engine with localized services. Wolfram Alpha Offers specialized knowledge in math, science, and data analysis. Archive.org Digital archive of old websites, videos, and music. PubMed Search for medical and life science research papers. YouTube The world's largest platform for video content. Ask.com Provides answers to simple questions in a user-friendly format. Startpage Displays Google results while maintaining anonymity. Qwant Popular in Europe for privacy-focused search results.
